## What is the Aarogyam Package?

**Aarogyam** is Thyrocare's flagship series of full body checkup packages — India's most popular preventive health screening profiles. The name combines the Hindi word for health (*Aarogya*) and the concept of wellness. These packages are processed at Thyrocare's NABL and CAP accredited laboratories, which test over 100,000 samples per day.

Aarogyam packages are available exclusively through Thyrocare's authorised collection partners — including **Ayropath** — with free home sample collection.

## Aarogyam Package Comparison

Here is a side-by-side comparison of the major Aarogyam variants:

### Aarogyam 1.1 (72 Parameters)

The original flagship. Covers the essential health departments comprehensively:

**Includes:** CBC (24 parameters), LFT (11), KFT (7), Thyroid (3 — TSH, T3, T4), Lipid Profile (8), Blood Sugar (3 — Fasting, HbA1c, Average Blood Glucose), Iron Studies (3), Urine Analysis (10)

**Best for:** Adults 25–45 seeking a thorough annual check. Ideal first-time health screening. No Vitamin D or B12.

**Fasting required:** Yes (10–12 hours)

---

### Aarogyam C (98 Parameters)

The advanced package. Adds Vitamins, electrolytes, and pancreatic function to Aarogyam 1.1.

**Additional over Aarogyam 1.1:** Vitamin D, Vitamin B12, Phosphorus, Calcium, Magnesium, Amylase (pancreatic enzyme), Globulin ratio

**Best for:** Adults 35+ or anyone with fatigue, muscle weakness, or Vitamin D/B12 suspicion. Vegetarians strongly benefit.

**Fasting required:** Yes (10–12 hours)

---

### Aarogyam Basic / Aarogyam B (64 Parameters)

A streamlined, budget-friendly option covering the essentials without thyroid panel.

**Includes:** CBC, LFT, KFT, Lipid Profile, Blood Sugar, Urine Analysis

**Best for:** Young adults (20–30) for a quick annual baseline. Budget-conscious families.

**Fasting required:** Yes

---

### Aarogyam Male (88 Parameters)

Aarogyam C **+ male-specific hormonal tests.**

**Additional:** Testosterone (Total), DHEA-Sulphate, PSA (Prostate Specific Antigen — critical cancer marker for men over 40)

**Best for:** Men above 35 with energy, libido, or weight concerns. Essential for prostate health screening from age 40.

---

### Aarogyam Female (88–92 Parameters)

Aarogyam C **+ female-specific hormonal tests.**

**Additional:** FSH, LH, Oestradiol, Prolactin (key hormones for menstrual health and fertility)

**Best for:** Women with PCOS, irregular periods, fertility concerns, or perimenopausal symptoms.
